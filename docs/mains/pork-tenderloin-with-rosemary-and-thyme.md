# Pork Tenderloin With Rosemary and Thyme

Lorraine Major<br/>
Julia, Jessica and Andrea Major

- 1 lb pork tenderloin
- 2 Tbsp Dijon mustard, approximately
- 2 tsp fresh rosemary

- 1/2 tsp thyme
- 1/4 tsp crushed black peppercorns, optional

Spread tenderloin generously with Dijon mustard. Place in roasting pan.  Sprinkle with remaining ingredients. Roast at 350Â°F for 35 to 45 minutes, or until no longer pink inside. To serve, cut into thin slices. Makes 3 servings.

Per Serving: 248 calories, 14 g fat.
