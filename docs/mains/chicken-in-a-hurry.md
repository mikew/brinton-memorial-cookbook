# Chicken In a Hurry

Janice Winsor<br/>
Andrew Winsor

- 1 chicken, cut up (or any chicken parts)
- 1/2 cup ketchup

- 1/4 cup water
- 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
- 1 envelope dry onion soup

Arrange chicken parts in small roaster. In bowl, combine remaining ingredients and mix well. Pour over chicken, covering all parts. Bake, covered, in a 350Â°F oven for 1 hour, until very tender. Especially good served over rice or potatoes. Serves 4 to 6.
