# Oven Barbecued Ribs

Nancy Stokes<br/>
Ben and Jane Stokes

- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 tsp dry mustard
- 1 clove garlic, crushed
- 1 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- 1 cup white vinegar

- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp pepper
- 3/4 cup ketchup
- Fresh pork spareribs
