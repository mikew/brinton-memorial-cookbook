# St. Phillips Barbecue Chicken Wings

Sarah Chalker

- 1 cup chopped onion
- 1 Knorr chicken cube
- 1/2 cup molasses
- 2 Tbsp cornstarch
- 2 tsp dry mustard

- 1 cup ketchup
- 1/4 cup brown sugar
- 3/4 cup vinegar
- Salt and pepper, to taste

Cut tips off wings. Place in casserole dish. Combine all remaining ingredients to make sauce. Pour over wings. Bake at 350Â°F for 1 1/2 hours. Great with rice and vegetables.
