# Salmon Teriyaki

Lois Hoegg<br/>
Charlotte, Catherine and Rachel Crosbie

- 2 lb salmon fillets
- 1 cup soya or tamari sauce
- 1/4 cup sherry

- 2 Tbsp sugar
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 Tbsp grated ginger

Marinate fish for a minimum of 45 minutes and up to a full day in a mixture of the remaining ingredients. Place in a greased casserole and bake at 450Â°F for 15 to 20 minutes, or until flaky. Serve with rice and your favourite vegetable. Garnish with parsley.
