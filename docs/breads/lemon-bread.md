# Lemon Bread

Tom Calon and Gerlinde VanDriel<br/>
Kirsten and Charlotte Calon

- 1/2 cup margarine
- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- Grated rind of 1 lemon
- 2 eggs
- 1 1/2 cups flour

- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/2 cup milk
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 1/2 cup sugar

Cream margarine and 1 1/2 cups sugar well. Add lemon rind and eggs.  Sift together flour, baking powder and salt. Add to creamed mixture. Stir in milk. Beat at high speed. Grease and flour 1 large loaf pan. Pour in batter. Bake at 375Â°F for 45 minutes. While still warm, glaze with lemon juice mixed with 1/2 cup sugar.
