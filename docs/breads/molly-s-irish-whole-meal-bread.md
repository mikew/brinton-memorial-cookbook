# Molly's Irish Whole Meal Bread

Anne Murray<br/>
Daniel Murray

- 12 oz whole meal flour or wheat and oat flour
- 4 oz bran
- 1 tsp sugar

- 2 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 3/4 pt buttermilk
- 2 Tbsp vegetable oil

Preheat oven to 400Â°F. Preheat baking pan in oven. Mix dry ingredients thoroughly. Stir in oil and buttermilk quickly. Turn mixture onto a floured board. Knead lightly 4 or 5 times. Add more flour if necessary. Flatten into an oval shape 1 inch thick. Place on floured and heated baking pan.  Draw a cross on top of bread dough. Bake for 40 to 45 minutes. The bread is baked when it sounds hollow when tapped. Wrap in a clean cloth and leave to cool on a wire rack.
