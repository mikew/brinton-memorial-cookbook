# Cinnamon Rolls

Lorna Berndt Piercey<br/>
Jamie and Caitlin Piercey

- 2 cups flour
- 4 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 cup margarine

- 1 cup milk
- Butter, softened
- Brown sugar
- Cinnamon

Mix flour, baking powder and salt. Cut in margarine until crumbs form.  Add milk and stir. Knead dough until uniform. Roll dough into a long rectanglular shape about 6 inches wide and 1/4 inch thick. Spread butter onto dough surface. Sprinkle with brown sugar and cinnamon. Roll up dough into a long cylinder. Slice into 1/2 inch thick circles. Bake at 425Â°F for 15 minutes.
