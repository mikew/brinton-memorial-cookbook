# Best Bran Muffins

Serena Hancock, Grade 1 Teacher
Peter and Robert Bazeley

- 1/4 cup sunflower oil
- 1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar
- 1/4 cup molasses
- 2 eggs, unbeaten
- 1 cup buttermilk
- 1 cup unbleached flour

- 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- Salt
- 1 1/2 cups natural wheat bran
- Chocolate chips
- Hulled sunflower seeds

Mix oil and sugar together. Add molasses and eggs and beat together well. Add buttermilk and then bran. Combine flour, baking powder, baking soda and a little salt. Add to liquid ingredients. Place in greased muffin cups. Top with chocolate chips and sunflower seeds. Bake at 400Â°F for 18 to 20 minutes. Makes 1 dozen large muffins. Recipe doubles nicely.
