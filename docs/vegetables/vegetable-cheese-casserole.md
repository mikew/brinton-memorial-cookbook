# Vegetable Cheese Casserole

Janet Parsons<br/>
Katelyn Parsons

- 1 pkg frozen mixed vegetables
- 1 pkg frozen chopped broccoli
- 1 can cream of mushroom soup

- 1 pkg shredded or sliced mozzarella cheese
- 3 crackers

Cook frozen vegetables according to package directions. Do not overcook. Drain. Put into an 8 inch casserole dish. Add soup. Stir. Put cheese on top. Crush crackers on top. Put into a 350Â°F oven for 1/2 to 3/4 hour, until bubbly and browning, longer if necessary. Great for barbecue or as a side dish or pot luck.
