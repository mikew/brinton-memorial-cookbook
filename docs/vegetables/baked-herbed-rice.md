# Baked Herbed Rice

Tom Calon and Gerlinde VanDriel<br/>
Kirsten and Charlotte Calon

- 3/4 tsp salt
- 3 pkg or cubes chicken bouillon
- 2 tsp chives

- 2 tsp parsley
- 1 cup rice
- 2 1/2 cups boiling water

Dissolve salt and chicken bouillon in boiling water. Add chives and parsley. Measure rice into a medium sized, casserole dish with snug-fitting lid. Pour liquid over rice and stir. Cover and bake at 350Â°F for 1 hour, without removing the lid. Fluff with a fork when done.
