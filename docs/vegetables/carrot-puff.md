# Carrot Puff

Debbie Patten<br/>
Juleah Patten

- 2 lb carrots
- 1/2 cup water
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 6 Tbsp butter
- 3/4 cup graham crumbs

- Dash ginger
- 2 eggs, separated
- 1/4 cup brown sugar
- 1/4 cup bread crumbs

Boil carrots in salted water for 15 minutes. Drain and mash. Combine carrots with 4 tablespoons butter, graham crumbs and ginger. Blend until smooth. Place in a tall casserole dish. Beat in egg yolks and set aside until cooled. In a separate bowl, beat egg whites until stiff. Fold into carrot mixture. Combine remaining 2 tablespoons butter, brown sugar and bread crumbs and sprinkle on top. Bake at 375Â°F for 45 minutes.
