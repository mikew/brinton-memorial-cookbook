# Mary Vickers

Lorna Proudfoot, Jeff Driscoll<br/>
Deidre and Shannon Driscoll

- 1 loaf sliced white bread
- 1/2 lb shredded cheddar cheese
- 6 slices crumbled crisp fried bacon
- 4 oz slivered almonds
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- Salt and pepper, to taste

Preheat oven to 400Â°F. Remove crusts from bread. Cut into strips and place on a cookie sheet. Blend all remaining ingredients in food processor. Spread over bread. You may freeze or bake for 10 minutes, until browned.
