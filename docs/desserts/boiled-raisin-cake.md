# Boiled Raisin Cake

Liz Anderson<br/>
Jessica Anderson

- 1 cup margarine
- 2 cups boiling water
- 1 box raisins
- 2 cups sugar

- 2 tsp cinnamon
- 2 tsp cloves
- 2 tsp baking soda
- 3 1/2 cups flour

Boil the first 6 ingredients together for 2 minutes. Let cool to room temperature. Add baking soda and flour and blend well. Spray a bundt or tube pan with Pam. Pour batter in and bake at 275Â°F for 1 1/2 hours.  Let sit for 15 minutes before removing from pan.
