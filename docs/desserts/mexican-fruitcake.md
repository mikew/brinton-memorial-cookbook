# Mexican Fruitcake

- 2 cups flour
- 2 cups sugar

- 1 cup chopped pecans or walnuts
- 19 oz can crushed pineapple with juice

Icing:

- 8 oz pkg cream cheese
- 1/2 cup butter
- 2 cups icing sugar
