# Almond Crescents

Glenda Ellsworth
Julia Bowdring

- 2 1/4 cups flour
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 1/4 cups butter, softened

- 1 cup icing sugar
- 2 tsp vanilla
- 1 cup ground almonds

Stir together flour and salt. Cream butter. Beat in sugar and vanilla. Add dry ingredients to creamed mixture, a little at a time. Add almonds last.  Form into 1 inch balls. Shape into crescents. Bake at 325Â°F for 12 to 15 minutes. Sprinkle with icing sugar while still warm.
