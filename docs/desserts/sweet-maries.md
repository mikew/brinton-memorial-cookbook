# Sweet Maries

Jennifer Tucker<br/>
Allison Tucker

*No bake*

- 1/2 cup corn syrup
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup peanut butter
- 1 Tbsp butter

- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1/2 cup unsalted peanuts
- 2 cups Rice Krispies
- Chocolate chips

Heat the first 5 ingredients in saucepan. Add peanuts and Rice Krispies.  Press into an 8x8 inch pan. Spread melted chocolate chips on top.
