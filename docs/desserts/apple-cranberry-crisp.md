# Apple Cranberry Crisp

Myra Hogan
Jennifer Hogan

- 6 medium apples, peeled, cored and quartered (approximately 4 cups)
- 1 cup cranberries
- 1/4 cup white sugar
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1 cup flour

- 1 cup rolled oats
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/2 cup butter

Place apples and cranberries in a mixing bowl. Add white sugar and 1 teaspoon cinnamon and toss together. Place in a 7x11 inch pan. Mix together flour, rolled oats, brown sugar, baking powder, 1 teaspoon cinnamon and salt. Rub in butter to form coarse crumbs. Spread over apples. Do not press. Bake in a 350Â°F oven for 30 minutes. Serve with cream or ice cream.
