# Whipped Shortbread

Jennifer Wyatt
Jessica and Michael Wyatt

- 1 lb butter
- 1 cup icing sugar
- 1/2 cup cornstarch

- 3 cups flour
- 1 tsp vanilla

In a large bowl, beat butter with electric mixer for about 5 minutes. Add vanilla. Combine dry ingredients and gradually add to butter, beating until light and fluffy. Drop by teaspoonsful onto greased cookie sheets.  Bake at 350Â° F for approximately 8 minutes, until slightly browned. Works great with cookie press too.
