# Granma's Molasses Cookies

Wanda Young<br/>
Tracy Young

- 1 cup margarine
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 egg
- 1 tsp cinnamon

- 1 tsp cloves
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/2 cup fresh milk
- 2 tsp baking soda
- 4 cups flour

Combine margarine, sugar, molasses and egg. Beat well. Add cinnamon, cloves and salt. Mix together milk and baking soda and stir into molasses mixture. Finally, stir in flour until well blended. Drop by heaping teaspoonsful onto cookie sheet. Bake at 350Â°F for 10 to 13 minutes.
